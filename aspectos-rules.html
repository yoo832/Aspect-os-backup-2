Thank you for reading such important information. AspectOS has a community full of wonderful people but sometimes, things can get crazy. Such as hate and/or spam. And we don't allow both of those. So we have laid some ground rules on what we expect for Aspect. If you do not agree to these terms, stop using AspectOS, AspectOS Jr., or AspectOS Pro.

WARNING: These are the rules for users. If you want to apply for AspectOS or are working for AspectOS, then read the Worker Rules.


What we Expect

We expect that you follow all rules that are displayed over here. To simplify this, do not hate on anybody and do not hate on AspectOS. Doing this will get you reported. We accept constructive criticism. This will help improve quality for AspectOS. We do not accept hate and harsh criticism. Doing this will not get your idea added. We believe that hate isn't always the answer to everything.


Ideas

Here at AspectOS, we sometimes struggle with new ideas. So we let the community give ideas to us. But we only accept ideas that are friendly to the community. What does this mean? This means that you shouldn't give ideas that encourages negative things. If you want to suggest an Idea to Aspect to implement in AspectOS, you need to know what we don't allow. Please read our Community Guidelines to see what and what we don't allow.


Personal Information

We do not collect your data according to our Privacy Policy and we make sure that your data is safe. But there are people on the Internet who can collect your data. Specifically personal information. Do not click links that look suspicious. We are not responsible for any harmful things that happen to your computer. Your actions have consequences and this message is to advise you that we have warned you.

Spamming

On our Official AspectOS Forum, we get a lot of off-topic things and we get spam. We do not encourage spam. Please do not spam our websites. Doing this makes the website unresponsive and we will have to fix it which will take a while. If you proceed to spam, we will report you on Scratch or temporarily disable your account. If the spam continues, we will have to possibly ban your account


Warnings

We have a strict system and if you break our rules, we will have to give you a warning. Of course some warnings will be temporary but some could be permanent if rules are broken constantly. Please review our Community Guidelines to prevent from this happening to you. Below is displaying how our warning system works. It starts off with a simple warning but more disipline is put into it every time. These are warnings if you do it on a daily basis. If you accidently violated the rules, you will just get a warning.

Violation 1: Warning

Violation 6: Muted for 1 week

Violation 7: Blocked for 1 day

Violation 8: Blocked for 1 week

Violation 13: IP Ban These violations only count against you if you have signed up for an account. People who do not sign in cannot access forums which means that these violations don't apply to them.


Accounts

These rules take effect in 2022. That same year, we will add a login system. That means users will be able to create an account with their own username and password. When an account is created, people will be able to go on the forums and talk to each other about bugs. They will also be able to save their changes for the website (Between Light and Dark Mode). Do not abuse accounts in anyway. Do not share your password with any others. Do not try to hack accounts. Doing this will get your account terminated. Do not fight with other accounts. We do not accept wild fighting and we will mute you if you enter a fight on the website.
